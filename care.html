Care & Training Tips

This page covers the basics for new owners. Always adjust to your dog’s age, health, and energy level.

Grooming

  • Brush several times a week to prevent mats.
  • Schedule professional grooming every 6–8 weeks.
  • Check ears weekly and keep nails trimmed.

Exercise

  • Plan on at least 45–60 minutes of daily activity.
  • Mix walks with play (fetch, tug) and short training sessions.
  • Use puzzle toys to burn mental energy.

Training

  • Keep sessions short, positive, and consistent.
  • Reinforce core cues: sit, down, stay, come, leave‑it.
  • Start socialization early and go at your dog’s pace.
