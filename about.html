About Goldendoodles

Goldendoodles are a mix between a Golden Retriever and a Poodle, known for being intelligent, affectionate, and great family companions. They have soft, wavy coats and friendly personalities that make them amazing pets.

Louis, the Goldendoodle featured on this site, is playful, curious, and full of personality!
