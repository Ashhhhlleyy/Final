About Goldendoodles

Goldendoodles are a cross between a Golden Retriever and a Poodle. They’re known for being social, smart, and eager to please. Many families like them for their friendly temperament and the possibility of lower shedding compared to some other breeds.

Personality & Traits

  • Social and affectionate with people and other dogs
  • Highly trainable and food‑motivated
  • Energetic and playful — daily exercise is key

Sizes & Coats

Goldendoodles come in a range of sizes (mini to standard) and coat types (wavy to curly). Coat care varies, but most will benefit from regular brushing and professional grooming.
